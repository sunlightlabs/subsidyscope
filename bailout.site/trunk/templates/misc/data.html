Downloadable Datasets

Subsidyscope maintains a variety of datasets. Some of them power the visualizations you see on the site, while others are used for behind-the-scene calculations. All of them are freely available under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution-Noncommercial 3.0 United States License.

On this page you can find links to the data products that we offer and descriptions of what they contain. Confused? Have a suggestion? Contact us and we'll be happy to answer your questions.

The Bailout

  • TARP Transactions
    (CSV)

    A list of the transactions occurring under the Treasury Department's Troubled Asset Relief Program. Includes purchases, repayments and dividends, as well as asset information for affected institutions.

    Updated approximately weekly, depending on Treasury's schedule and variations in their formatting.

  • FDIC Bank Failures
    (CSV)

    The bank failures charged to the FDIC's Deposit Insurance Fund. Includes bank name, location, closing date, estimated charge to the DIF and the URL of the associated IG report, if available.

    Updated intermittently — approximately 1-2 times per month.

  • FDIC QBP Snapshot
    (CSV)

    Data from the FDIC's Quarterly Banking Profile, a summary of financial results for all FDIC-insured institutions.

    Updated quarterly.

  • FDIC TLGP Participation
    (CSV)

    The banks that have specifically declared their status within the FDIC's TLGP program.

    Updated as information becomes available.

  • Federal Reserve H.4.1 Snapshot
    (CSV)

    The Federal Reserve's weekly statement of factors affecting its balances.

    Updated every Thursday.
