Subsidyscope

Downloadable Datasets

Subsidyscope maintains a variety of datasets. Some of them power the visualizations you see on the site, while others are used for behind-the-scene calculations. All of them are available for use under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution-Noncommercial 3.0 United States License.

On this page you can find links to the data products that we offer and descriptions of what they contain. Confused? Have a suggestion? Contact us and we'll be happy to answer your questions.

The Bailout

Subsidyscope maintains a number of datasets related to the government's unprecedented intervention in the financial sector.
  • TARP Transactions
    (CSV)

    A list of the transactions occurring under the Treasury Department's Troubled Asset Relief Program. Includes purchases, repayments and dividends, as well as asset information for affected institutions.

    Updated approximately weekly, depending on Treasury's schedule and variations in their formatting

  • FDIC Bank Failures
    (CSV)

    The bank failures charged to the FDIC's Deposit Insurance Fund. Includes bank name, location, closing date, estimated charge to the DIF and the URL of the associated IG report, if available.

    Updated intermittently — approximately 1-2 times per month

  • FDIC QBP Snapshot
    (CSV)

    Data from the FDIC's Quarterly Banking Profile, a summary of financial results for all FDIC-insured institutions.

    Updated quarterly

  • FDIC TLGP Participation
    (CSV)

    The banks that have specifically declared their status within the FDIC's TLGP program.

    Updated as information becomes available

  • Federal Reserve H.4.1 Snapshot
    (CSV)

    The Federal Reserve's weekly statement of factors affecting its balances.

    Updated every Thursday at 6 p.m. EST

Transportation

Subsidyscope is in the process of releasing the data supporting its work on the transportation sector. Please check back here often for updates. If you would like to ask us a specific question about our planned offerings, please use the contact form.
  • Airport Improvement Program (AIP)
    (Gzipped CSVs)

    Data from SubsidyScope's searchable AIP database. Original data has been parsed from spreadsheets available on the FAA website.

    Updated as information becomes available

  • CFDA/FAADS
    (Gzipped SQLite database, approx. 50M)

    The data powering our program directory and direct expenditure search. The former is extracted from PDFs and CFDA.gov; the latter portion is a transportation-oriented subset of data from USASpending.gov's FAADS dataset.

    Updated periodically; last update 10/23/2009

  • Export/Import Bank (Ex-Im)
    (CSV)

    Loans and loan guarantees made by the Export-Import Bank from FY 2007 to the present.

    Updated as information becomes available
