OBJECTIVES:                                                                           
  A cash grant alternative to the Special Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)         
  (Formally known as the Food Stamp Program) to improve diets of needy persons        
  residing in the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.                                        
TYPES OF ASSISTANCE:                                                                  
  Direct Payments for Specified Use.                                                  
USES AND USE RESTRICTIONS:                                                            
  Households receive money to purchase food.                                          
Applicant Eligibility:

  The Commonwealth of Puerto Rico alone is eligible.
