Subsidyscope maintains a variety of datasets. Some of them power the visualizations you see on the site, while others are used for behind-the-scene calculations. All of them are available for use under the terms of the Creative Commons Attribution-Noncommercial 3.0 United States License.

If you reference Subsidyscope in another publication please include a link to the main page (http://subsidyscope.org/) or include a citation such as this:

    Subsidyscope.org. The Pew Charitable Trusts. Washington, DC. Accessed Month, XX, 20XX. Retrieved from http://subsidyscope.org.

On this page you can find links to the data products that we offer and descriptions of what they contain. Confused? Have a suggestion? Contact us and we'll be happy to answer your questions.

Housing

  • CFDA/FAADS
    (Gzipped MySQL database export, approx. 130M)

    The data powering our program directory and direct expenditure search. The former is extracted from PDFs and CFDA.gov; the latter portion is a housing-oriented subset of data from USASpending.gov's FAADS dataset.

    Updated periodically; last update 3/30/11

  • FPDS
    (Gzipped MySQL database export, approx. 100M)

    The data powering our contract search. An energy-oriented subset of data from USASpending.gov's FPDS dataset.

    Updated periodically; last update 3/30/11

Transportation

  • Airport Improvement Program (AIP)
    (Gzipped CSVs)

    Data from SubsidyScope's searchable AIP database. Original data has been parsed from spreadsheets available on the FAA website.

    Last update 10/1/2009

  • CFDA/FAADS
    (Gzipped MySQL database export, approx. 130M)

    The data powering our program directory and direct expenditure search. The former is extracted from PDFs and CFDA.gov; the latter portion is a transportation-oriented subset of data from USASpending.gov's FAADS dataset.

    Updated periodically; last update 9/9/2010

  • Export/Import Bank (Ex-Im)
    (CSV)

    Loans and loan guarantees made by the Export-Import Bank from FY 2007 to the present.

    Last update 6/26/2009

  • Amtrak Route Performance
    (CSV)

    Route performance data for Amtrak, fiscal year 2008. See the Amtrak Route Performance Table for description and methodology.

    Last update 10/26/2009

  • Highway Funding Sources (1957-2007)
    (CSV)

    Funding sources for highways grouped by "user", "non-user" and "bonds". Includes nominal and inflation adjusted figures in 2007 dollars using the Construction Cost Index.

    Last update 11/23/2009

  • Highway Revenue Allocations (1957-2007)
    (CSV)

    Revenue from highway sources and allocations to highway and non-highway uses. Includes nominal and inflation adjusted figures in 2007 dollars using the Construction Cost Index.

    Last update 11/23/2009

  • MARAD Title XI Commitments and Defaults (1994-2009)
    (CSV)

    List of MARAD Title XI loan guarantees between 1994 and 2009. Data from Department of Transportation Inspector General (IG) reports on defaults between 1998 and 2009. Guarantees are recorded in the fiscal year of the commitment. Losses are recorded in the fiscal year of the default, as reported by the IG.

    Last update 11/23/2009

The Bailout

Subsidyscope maintains a number of datasets related to the government's unprecedented intervention in the financial sector.
  • TARP Transactions
    (CSV)

    A list of the transactions occurring under the Treasury Department's Troubled Asset Relief Program. Includes purchases, repayments and dividends, as well as asset information for affected institutions.

  • FDIC Bank Failures
    (CSV)

    The bank failures charged to the FDIC's Deposit Insurance Fund. Includes bank name, location, closing date, estimated charge to the DIF and the URL of the associated IG report, if available.

  • FDIC QBP Snapshot
    (CSV)

    Data from the FDIC's Quarterly Banking Profile, a summary of financial results for all FDIC-insured institutions.

  • FDIC TLGP Participation
    (CSV)

    The banks that have specifically declared their status within the FDIC's TLGP program.

  • Federal Reserve H.4.1 Snapshot
    (CSV)

    The Federal Reserve's weekly statement of factors affecting its balances.

Miscellaneous Data

  • Depreciation Data from IRS Statistics of Income
    (Excel) | (ODS) 84KB

    This data aggregates the numbers reported on IRS Form 4562, grouped by NAICS code and presents the total claimed depreciation value by asset class, grouped by industry.

    Source: Tax Year 2007 Corporation Statistics of Income

    Last update 4/13/2011; data as of 7/30/2010
